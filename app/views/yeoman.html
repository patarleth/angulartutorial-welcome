Before we get too deep into this, I feel an important issue must be addressed -

What the hell is Yeoman?

    "THE WEB'S SCAFFOLDING TOOL FOR MODERN WEBAPPS"
They love teh caps over there... Yeoman is a command line tool used to generate 400+ different kinds of web projects - I've pre-installed the angular-generator for this demo, but checkout the other community generators available to yo at http://yeoman.io/generators.html.

And of course - in your infinite spare time go check out https://github.com/yeoman/generator-angular
