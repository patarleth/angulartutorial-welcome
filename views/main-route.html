controller/view/route

definitions
  • controller - you puts your codes here
  • view - HTML "template" displaying the datas

  • route - mechanism to link between controllers and views, enabling links like

    <a href="http://foo.bar.com/index.html#/route-goes-here">text for link goes here</a>

    a route requires two corresponding files in your application

    • app/scripts/controllers/route-goes-here.js
    • app/views/route-goes-here.html
